Home Page

The home page of our website is just picutures of cute dogs and puppies to help you out on sad days. The second page of our website is picutures of dogs that are in need of adoption and a little information on each of them.

All dogs and puppies deserve to go to good homes. Homes where people will love and protect them.

Things to think about:

  • Can you take care of a dog?
  • Is there enough room where you live for the dog to be happy?
  • Do you have enough money to take care of all the dogs needs?
  • Do you live in a safe environment for the dog?
  • If the dog is special needs will you be able to fufill all of it's needs?
